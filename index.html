The specialty of Raja Ampat


The Raja Ampat Islands are in the westernmost part of Papua and stretch over an area of ​​approximately 4.6 million hectares. Raja Ampat Regency consists of 4 large islands, namely Waigeo, Batanta, Salawati and Misool Islands and 1,847 other small islands.
